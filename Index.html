How to Play

1. Select your difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard, or Expert)

2. Click on the letters in the correct order to form the hidden word

3. Click "Check Word" to verify your answer

4. Use "Shuffle" to rearrange the letters if needed

5. Earn points for each correct word - higher levels give more points!
